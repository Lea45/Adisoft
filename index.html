Modern luxury design within centuries-old building

Two houses with 7 floors connected into one home.

Located in the heart of Old Town Rovinj, Malipiero Residence was meticulously restored. Originally built by the Malipiero family, it dates back more than 300 years. The Malipiero family crest still adorns the house, as it does the Palazzo Malipiero on the Grand Canal in Venice.

It sits along Rovinj's old city wall, part of which is displayed on the main staircase. The Residence, with its 7 floors, delivers a unique sense of history combined with modern luxury design. The only five-star Residence in the Old Town of Rovinj with the privacy of a holiday home and personalized services of exceptional quality.
